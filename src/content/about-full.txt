Our Story

Every successful organization begins with an idea. At INTERNMITRA, our journey began with a friendship.

The story of INTERNMITRA is the story of two friends, Amarjeet Kumar and Premchand Nirala, whose friendship started in Class 6. Growing up together, they studied side by side, shared ambitions, solved challenges together, and dreamed of creating something that could make a meaningful impact on society.

As they progressed through higher education, they witnessed a challenge faced by students across India.

Although universities increasingly required internships as part of their academic curriculum, finding genuine internship opportunities remained difficult. Many students struggled to identify reliable organizations, understand internship requirements, prepare reports, or gain meaningful practical experience. For students from rural areas and Tier-2 and Tier-3 cities, these challenges were even greater due to limited awareness, fewer industry connections, and lack of proper guidance.

They met countless students who wanted to learn but didn’t know where to begin. Some had strong academic records but lacked confidence in interviews. Others possessed talent but had never worked on real-world projects. Many simply needed the right mentor and an opportunity to prove themselves.

These experiences inspired a question:

“Why should practical learning be a privilege for only a few students when it can become an opportunity for everyone?”

The answer to this question became the foundation of INTERNMITRA.

Instead of accepting these problems as inevitable, Amarjeet Kumar and Premchand Nirala decided to build a platform that would connect education with industry, theory with practice, and students with opportunities.

What began as a shared dream between two lifelong friends gradually evolved into INTERNMITRA Technologies Private Limited—an organization dedicated to empowering students through structured internships, practical education, and career-focused learning.

Today, INTERNMITRA is more than a company. It represents a mission to make quality internships accessible, affordable, and impactful for students across India.

Our Learning Methodology

At INTERNMITRA, we believe students learn best through experience.

Every internship follows a structured learning model:

Students complete registration and receive orientation about the internship structure, expectations, and learning outcomes.

Interactive Learning Sessions

Expert-led sessions combine theoretical concepts with real-world examples and industry practices.

Practical Assignments

Students apply concepts through assignments that reflect workplace scenarios.

Live Projects

Participants work on real or simulated projects to develop hands-on experience.

Continuous Mentorship

Dedicated mentors guide students throughout the internship, providing regular feedback and professional support.

Assessments

Students are evaluated through quizzes, assignments, presentations, and project submissions.

Final Evaluation

Performance is reviewed based on participation, project quality, professionalism, and overall learning outcomes.

Eligible participants receive internship-related credentials upon successful completion of program requirements.
